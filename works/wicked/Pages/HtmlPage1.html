About Wicked

Wicked is a musical based on the 1995 novel by Gregory Maguire, which is a retelling of the classic story of the Wizard of Oz from the perspective of the witches of the Land of Oz.

It tells the story of the unlikely friendship between two witches, Elphaba and Glinda, and explores themes of friendship, love, and the fight for justice.

Main Characters

  • Elphaba – The Wicked Witch of the West, who is misunderstood and fights for justice.
  • Glinda – The Good Witch, who starts as Elphaba’s friend but is later at odds with her.
  • The Wizard – A mysterious and manipulative figure in the Land of Oz.
  • Fiyero – A love interest for both Elphaba and Glinda.

Popular Songs

  • "Defying Gravity" – A powerful anthem sung by Elphaba.
  • "For Good" – A heartwarming duet between Elphaba and Glinda.
  • "Popular" – A fun, bubbly number sung by Glinda.
